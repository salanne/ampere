The AMPERE project was funded between 2018 and 2023 by the European Research Council (ERC) under the European Union's Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme (grant agreement No. 771294).

AMPERE aimed at Accounting for Metallicity, Polarization of the Electrolyte, and Redox reactions in computational Electrochemistry.

Applications focused on the discovery of new electrolytes for energy storage, such as

(1) ‘‘Water-in-salts’’ to understand why these new liquids enable much higher voltage than conventional solutions

(2) Redox reactions inside a nanoporous electrode to support the development of future capacitive energy storage devices
